What's Included

Every submission includes

No hidden fees. No upsells. Everything you need is included in every tier.

🤖 AI + Human Dual Grading

Our AI system scans and grades first. A certified human grader then independently reviews. Both must agree before a grade is issued.

📊 Full Sub-Score Breakdown

Centering, edges, corners, and surface — all published with your grade. You'll know exactly how every point was earned or lost.

🔒 Premium Slab Encapsulation

UV-resistant, tamper-evident, optically clear slabs. Engineered for decades of protection, not just display.

📱 NFC + QR Verification

Dual verification built into every slab. Scan with any phone — no app required — to instantly verify authenticity and grade.

🌐 Digital Certificate

A permanent, publicly accessible digital record of your card's grade, sub-scores, and authentication status.

📦 Secure Handling

Your cards are tracked and handled with care at every step. Fully insured while in our facility.
