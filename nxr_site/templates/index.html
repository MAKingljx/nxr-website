Our Approach

AI + Human = Better Grades

We don't trust robots alone. We don't trust humans alone. But together? That's the sweet spot.

1

AI First Pass

Our AI scans your card with micron-level precision, analyzing centering, edges, corners, and surface. It's like having a microscope with a PhD.

2

Human Review

A certified human grader independently reviews the AI's analysis. If they disagree, the card goes to a senior grader. No ego, just accuracy.

3

Cross-Verification

Both must agree before a final grade is issued. If they don't, we document why. Transparency isn't a feature — it's the whole point.
