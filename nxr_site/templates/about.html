About NXR

Built by collectors.
For collectors.

We started NXR because we were tired of the same thing you were tired of — grades you couldn't trust, institutions that didn't care, and a system that rewarded loyalty over accuracy.

Our Mission

Transparency isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

NXR was founded on a simple belief: collectors deserve to know exactly how their cards are graded. Not just the final number — the full breakdown, the criteria, the reasoning.

We combine AI-powered precision scanning with certified human expertise to deliver grades that are consistent, documented, and defensible. Every time. For every card. Regardless of who submitted it.

We don't have a VIP tier. We don't have a fast lane for high-profile submissions. Your card gets the same rigorous process as everyone else's. That's not a policy — it's a principle.

1

Transparency over tradition

Every sub-score published. Every criterion documented. No black box, no mystery grades.

2

Consistency over convenience

The same standards applied to every card, every time. No exceptions for high-value submissions.

3

Technology in service of accuracy

AI doesn't replace human judgment — it removes human bias. Both work together.

4

Collectors first, always

We exist to serve the people who love these cards — not the institutions that profit from them.
